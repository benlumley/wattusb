Live wattage in your menu bar

wattusb

See exactly how many watts your Mac is pulling.

A tiny macOS menu bar app for USB-C charging power. Instantly tell whether a charger, or which port on a multi-port charger, is actually giving you good juice.

Under the hood

How it works

All data comes straight from macOS's AppleSmartBattery IOKit service.

  • Live input draw: PowerTelemetryData.SystemPowerIn, the watts flowing in from the adapter.
  • PD contract: AdapterDetails gives the voltage/current the charger negotiated as its max.
  • Battery side: Voltage × Amperage reveals how much is actually reaching the cells.
  • Refresh: every 2 seconds. The title uses the menu-bar font so it lines up with Control Center.
